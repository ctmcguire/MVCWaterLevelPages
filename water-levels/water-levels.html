The MVCA monitors water levels and flow at 40 sites in the Mississippi and Carp river watersheds. Data from individual locations can be found below the map. Automated gauging sites are updated daily while manual staff gauge readings are taken weekly.

As of January 1, 2017, the MVCA is no longer operating or monitoring levels at the Clayton Lake dam.  For information regarding water levels on Clayton Lake or flows on the Indian River, please contact the Ministry of Natural Resources and Forestry, Kemptville office at 613-258-8204.

Please take note that all data is provisional. See the disclaimer below.
