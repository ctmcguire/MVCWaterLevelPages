Check out lake and stream levels updated daily

The MVCA monitors 41 water level and flow gauges in the Mississippi Valley Watershed. Please use the links below to view gauge readings from individual locations. Gauge readings are updated both daily and weekly and can be viewed over time in both table format or line graph layout.

As of January 1, 2017, the MVCA is no longer monitoring levels or operating the Clayton Lake dam.  For information regarding water levels on Clayton Lake or flows on the Indian River, please contact the Ministry of Natural Resources and Forestry, Kemptville office at 613-258-8204.

Levels are provided in Meters Above Sea Level (MASL).  All flows are provided in cubic meters per second (cms).  Precipitation is recorded in millimeters (mm).

Please take note that all data is provisional. See Disclaimer.
